What is CodeTrace?

CodeTrace analyzes source code to find structural and logical similarities between different submissions. It helps detect copied or slightly modified programs that are hard to catch manually.

By comparing patterns across assignments, the system highlights suspicious matches so instructors and students can review and maintain academic integrity with confidence.
